@startuml
left to right direction

' --- שחקנים בצד שמאל ---
actor Client
actor "Club Member" as ClubMember
actor "Administrator" as Admin

' כל Club Member הוא סוג מיוחד של Client
Client <|-- ClubMember

rectangle "ParkWise System" {

  ' --- ניהול חניונים ולקוחות ---
  usecase "Manage Parking Lots\n& Details" as UC_ManageLots
  usecase "Register to Club Membership" as UC_Club
  usecase "Select Preferred\nParking Lots" as UC_SelectLots

  ' --- כניסה לחניון ---
  usecase "Detect Vehicle\nArrival/Departure" as UC_CheckVehicle
  usecase "Follow Parking Lot\nInstructions" as UC_FollowInstr
  usecase "Open Gate" as UC_OpenGate

  ' --- חניה בפועל ---
  usecase "Assign Conveyor\nand Parking Space" as UC_Assign
  usecase "Update Vehicle Location" as UC_UpdateLocation
  usecase "Transfer Vehicle" as UC_TransferVehicle

  ' --- סיום חניה ותשלום ---
  usecase "End Parking and Pay" as UC_EndParking
  usecase "Process Payment" as UC_ProcessPayment
  usecase "Issue Receipt" as UC_Receipt

  ' --- תעריפים ודוחות ---
  usecase "Update Price Lists" as UC_UpdatePrices
  usecase "Generate Annual\nSummary Report" as UC_AnnualReport
  usecase "Generate Customer\nClub Growth Report" as UC_ClubReport
}

' --- שחקנים בצד ימין ---
actor "Gate Sensor" as GS
actor "Conveyors Controller" as CC
actor "Conveyor" as Conveyor
actor "Payment Gateway" as PG
actor "Integrated Rate Service" as IRS

' --- קשרי שחקנים ---

Admin --> UC_ManageLots
Admin --> UC_AnnualReport
Admin --> UC_ClubReport

Client --> UC_EndParking
Client --> UC_Club
Client --> UC_FollowInstr

ClubMember --> UC_SelectLots

GS --> UC_CheckVehicle

CC --> UC_Assign

GS --> UC_OpenGate

Conveyor --> UC_TransferVehicle

IRS --> UC_UpdatePrices

PG --> UC_ProcessPayment

' --- קשרי include / extend עיקריים ---

' כשלון בבדיקת רכב -> הוראות ללקוח
UC_CheckVehicle <.. UC_FollowInstr : <<extend>> 

' הצלחה בבדיקת רכב -> פתיחת שער
UC_CheckVehicle <.. UC_OpenGate : <<extend>> 

' הקצאת מסוע ומקום חניה כוללת עדכון מיקום רכב
UC_Assign --> UC_UpdateLocation : <<include>>

' בסיום חניה מחשבים תשלום, משלמים ומוציאים קבלה
UC_EndParking --> UC_ProcessPayment : <<include>>
UC_EndParking <.. UC_Receipt : <<extend>>
@enduml
